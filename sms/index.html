SMS & Real-Time Tracking

How ride notifications and live tracking work for Sunline Shuttle.

Ride tracking is powered by SlyTrac, which generates a unique link for each trip - letting you see your driver's live location and estimated arrival time right in your text messages.

SMS notifications are sent automatically at two moments: when your ride is booked and confirmed, and again when your driver is almost there.

Tracking notifications are sent from: 1 (844) 510-0179
